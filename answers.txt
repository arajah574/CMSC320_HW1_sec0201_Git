Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git helps us track our changes through allowing us to go back to 
previous versions of our code and collaborate on it.

 Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging creates a merge commit that integrates changes. Rebasing 
integrates the changes through reapplying a bunch of commits
